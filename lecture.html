Facts from Related Lessons:
  • Previewing Details : You get an idea of what you will find in the text. Your brain begins making connections and your comprehension will be better and faster. You pick up a great deal of information about the text.
  • Previewing Introduction : Previewing is finding out what you are going to be reading before you actually read.
  • Scanning Details : It’s a high speed reading. When you scan, you have a specific question in mind and you look for specific piece of information.
  • Scanning Tips : Don’t read every word. Let your eyes move quickly across the page until you find what you are looking for. Use clues on the page to help you.
  • Skimming Details : It’s a fast reading technique. It is used to obtain the gist of a piece of text.
